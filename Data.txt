##  Key Configurations
- Installed AD DS and promoted server to Domain Controller
- Configured DNS for domain name resolution
- Created OUs, users, and security groups
- Joined client machines to the domain
- Applied Group Policy Objects (GPOs):
  - Password Policy
  - Disable Control Panel (HR)
  - Drive Mapping (Sales)
  - Screen Lock Timeout
